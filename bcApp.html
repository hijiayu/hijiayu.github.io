Block Charging

Charging Station Management for Apartment Residents

Background

With the rapid development of electric vehicles' market, people gradually care about how necessary is the electric vehicle charging station. We were commissioned to develop an APP and Web content management system for an apartment complex. The superintendents could check and manage the state of the electric meters and the charging station for the inhabitants.

Goal

Finish App and CMS development within 3 months

Challenge

There are two companies are tendering for the software development contract, another one is a famous brand of public charging station.

Outcome
