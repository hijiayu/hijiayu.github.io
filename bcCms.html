Block Charging

Manage Charging Station for Apartment Committee by CMS

Background

With the rapid development of electric vehicles' market, people gradually care about how necessary is the electric vehicle charging station. We were commissioned to develop an APP and Web content management system for an apartment complex. The superintendents could check and manage the state of the electric meters and the charging station for the inhabitants.

Goal

Finish App and CMS development within 3 months

Challenge

  • There are two companies are tendering for the software development contract, another one is a famous brand of public charging station.
  • A back-end engineer and I was in charge of this project, and the producers are only us. And I have to proposal design for the APP and CMS at the same time.

    • Gaols

  • Users can finish the progress of the App from searching the parking to paying by credit card effectively
  • Build the App in 6 months, and launch in the App store

    • Solution

    After confirming requests from the owner, I discussed the priority of function pages with the salesman, engineers. I chose Bootstrap 5 admin template to customize to match my design. It’s convenient to save time and design motion interaction. Although I could create a website page layout in HTML CSS by myself, but it would take me much time to search the motion effects related to Javascript.

    Design

    Outcome

    Although the development proposal is finished on time, this case of owner changed his mind in the middle of a commission.
